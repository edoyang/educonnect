ABOUT US

Built by students, for students

EduConnect is a student-driven platform designed to make academic life easier through shared resources, collaboration tools, and campus event tracking.

Our Mission

To empower students with easy access to academic resources and collaborative tools that enhance the learning experience and foster community.

Our Vision

A connected academic community where every student can find the help they need, collaborate effortlessly, and achieve their full potential.

Our Values

Accessibility, collaboration, and innovation guide everything we do. We believe learning is better when it's shared.

OUR STORY

How EduConnect Started

EduConnect was born out of frustration. As university students, we found ourselves constantly searching across multiple platforms for lecture notes, struggling to coordinate study groups, and missing important campus events.

We realized that students needed a single, unified platform that brings together academic resources, collaboration tools, and event management. That's how EduConnect came to life — a platform built with real student needs in mind.

Today, EduConnect serves as a hub where students can access curated study materials, form study groups with peers, and stay organized with an integrated events calendar. Our goal is simple: help students learn, collaborate, and succeed together.
